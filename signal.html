under the hood
WebAudio API + AnalyserNode microphone input is routed through an AnalyserNode with a 2048-point FFT. getByteTimeDomainData() and getByteFrequencyData() are called every frame to read the live waveform and spectrum.
Canvas 2D perspective projection each waveform sample is treated as a 3D point, rotated around the Y axis, and projected to screen with a focal-length perspective formula, all in Canvas 2D.
frequency-driven color bass energy (bins 1–12, ~0–260Hz) drives the red channel. high-frequency energy (bins 150–350, ~3–7kHz) drives teal. the gradient recomputes every frame and follows the 3D rotation.
decay + persistence a smoothed RMS envelope drives an amplitude decay scalar — loud input snaps to 1 in ~12 frames, silence fades to 0 over ~5 seconds. a semi-transparent fill each frame creates motion trails.
